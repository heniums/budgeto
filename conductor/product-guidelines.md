# Product Guidelines: Budgeto

## Prose Style
- **Conversational & encouraging.** Copy speaks to the user in a friendly, approachable voice. Celebrate small wins (e.g., "Nice! You're 10% under budget this month") and guide gently when something needs attention.
- Keep microcopy short; avoid jargon.

## Branding
- **Trustworthy palette.** Use calm greens and blues that evoke stability and financial growth. Maintain a restrained, confident look rather than loud or playful colors.

## UX Principles
- **Fast entry.** Core tasks — especially adding an expense — must be reachable in 1–2 taps/clicks. Reduce friction on the most frequent actions.
- Prefer sensible defaults so users can act first and configure later.

## Accessibility
- **WCAG AA compliance.** Ensure sufficient color contrast, full keyboard navigation, and properly labeled controls and inputs. Do not rely on color alone to convey meaning.

## General
- Consistency over cleverness: reuse components and patterns.
- Respect the user's data; never show alarming or ambiguous messages about their money.
